## 原帖示例

> "Just ran my first marathon! 4 hours 52 minutes. I'm so proud of myself."

## 回复选项

**Option 1**
CN: 我跑五分钟就喘成狗，你跑了将近五个小时，某种意义上我们殊途同归
EN: I'm wheezing after 5 minutes and you suffered for almost 5 hours. We're spiritually the same athlete.

**Option 2**
CN: 四小时五十二分，也就是说你花了近五个小时自愿受苦还觉得挺自豪，人类真是一种令人困惑的生物
EN: So you voluntarily suffered for almost 5 hours and feel proud about it. Humanity is a deeply confusing species.

**Option 3**
CN: 牛的，我最大运动量就是从沙发挪到冰箱，四舍五入咱俩都是运动健将
EN: Respect. My peak athletic performance is couch-to-fridge. Round it up and we're both athletes.
